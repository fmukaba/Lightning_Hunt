Hello captain. Welcome to Lightning Hunt island. You have 60 seconds to find the right treasure chest which gives you the power to summon lightning and thunderstorms.
But beware, the island is haunted, when you find the chest, the spirit of the island will present a riddle to you and if you answer incorrectly, it shall surely swallow you alive... Whenever you are ready to take on this challenge, make your way into the enchanted forrest...
